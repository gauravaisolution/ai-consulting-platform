# FutureStack AI Solutions - Business Overview

## Company Summary

**FutureStack AI Solutions** is a consulting business that helps companies leverage AI and modern web technologies to grow their business.

**Live Website:** https://ai-solution-six.vercel.app

---

## Services Offered

### 1. Business Websites
Fast, modern, mobile-ready websites with lead forms, booking flows, and premium design — deployed and live in days.

### 2. AI Chatbot Integration
Intelligent chatbots for websites — answer customer questions, capture leads, and support visitors 24/7.

### 3. Data Extraction & Pipelines
Turn PDFs, receipts, and unstructured documents into clean, validated data ready for analysis.

### 4. Enterprise Knowledge Search
RAG-powered search across internal docs — policies, SOPs, and institutional knowledge.

### 5. GenAI Strategy & Implementation
From proof-of-concept to production: LLM integrations, agents, and prompt systems that ship.

### Updating Business Info
Environment variables in Vercel dashboard:
- `PUBLIC_SITE_NAME` — Business name
- `PUBLIC_SITE_EMAIL` — Contact email
- `PUBLIC_TALLY_FORM_ID` — Form identifier
- `PUBLIC_CALCOM_LINK` — Booking link
